Legacy Wealth

A clean, functional, and sleek website for Legacy Wealth, showcasing their impactful financial strategies and guidance for enhancing clients' financial well-being.

Role

Website Designer and Developer

Responsibilities

Website Design, Image Saving and Optimization, Website Development, Custom CSS, Animation, Launch

This project was a collaboration with brand specialist Megan King. Megan and her team crafted the brand identity for Legacy Wealth, while I designed and developed the website.



The client requested a functional, sleek, and clean website that allows for easy content editing while maintaining a visually appealing design.

The website emphasizes the core values of Legacy Wealth — passion, ambition, vision, and community. With a minimalist design, it balances dark colors with a light, user-friendly interface, reflecting the founder’s commitment to making financial literacy accessible and inviting to all.

To enhance the website's engagement, I incorporated hover effects and animations, adding dynamic elements that bring the site to life.

Xcelerator

A dynamic and engaging website for Xcelerator, a summit that connects business owners with top industry leaders to accelerate growth and drive success.

Role

Website Designer and Developer

Responsibilities

Graphic Design, Website Design, Image Saving and Optimization, Wix Website Development, Launch

A former client from one of my previous projects reached out for assistance in designing and developing a website for Xcelerator, a business summit hosted by business and numbers expert Martha Razo, aimed at top industry leaders and successful entrepreneurs.



The goal of the website is to attract more attendees, speakers, and sponsors by enhancing the appeal of this already exciting event. The design focuses on showcasing the event’s prominence, featuring logos of notable sites where it has been featured, as well as logos of the event’s sponsors.

All essential information about the event is included and easily accessible, with a gallery highlighting images from last year’s event to give visitors a clear idea of what to expect.

This client has entrusted me with designing both print and online promotional materials before starting on the website, making this site an extension of those initial designs.

FC Media

Eye-catching and inventive scroll-stopper thumbnails for FC Media, a channel dedicated to entrepreneurs, businesses, and the triumphs and challenges of influential figures.

Role

Thumbnail and Social Media Designer

Responsibilities

Ideation, Design, AI Image Generation

Working with this client has really allowed me to explore my creativity and translate my ideas into something that is appealing to the viewers.



Each thumbnail is an opportunity to tell a story and make a statement. It serves as a trailer to what they can expect from the video. Here's a brief example from a thumbnail I made for a video that tells the story of Alcoholics Anonymous.

This before-and-after showcase highlights the creative journey of turning rough ideas into eye-catching thumbnails. It starts with brainstorming and rough drafts, moves through several rounds of tweaks, and finishes with polished and detailed graphics.

This particular channel focuses on business mini-documentaries, ranging from success stories to dirty secrets, politics, and downfalls.


As a glimpse to the creative process of thumbnail creation, here is a brief explanation behind the YouTube thumbnail featuring a young billionaire named Palmer Luckey.

With the implementation of his famous electrocution story, in the design, he appears to be electrocuted by a coin with Mark Zuckerberg's face on it, a nod to the turning point of his career when Mark purchased Oculus for 2 billion dollars.


The scattered money and Oculus symbolize his most notable achievements, while the Hawaiian shirt, a staple of Luckey's, adds a playful touch to the design.


The drones flying in the background and the stamps on his face represent his current venture with Anduril Industries, a defense company that builds innovative weapons and surveillance equipment.


The word "Cancelled" on the stamps alludes to the controversy that caused him to lose the support of VR game developers and eventually his job at Facebook.

Every thumbnail demonstrates the thought and dedication put into each design to ensure the best possible output for clients.

Scar Alchemy

An Instagram template package for Scar Alchemy, a specialist in paramedical and cosmetic tattooing for dark skin and people of color.

Role

Social Media Designer

Responsibilities

Design

The task for this project was to create three Instagram post designs and corresponding story graphics. The theme and aesthetic were centered around Alchemy, with the client providing a helpful style reference to guide the designs.

To complete the project, the initial step involved immersing in the theme and inspiration provided by the client to ensure alignment with their vision. Incorporating the brand's colors helped maintain consistency with the established identity. To amplify the magical ambiance, a glow effect was added to the text.

In response to the client's request, visual elements such as the moon, torn paper edges, and golden stars were integrated into the before-after design. Additionally, photos were presented on a tarot-like card to enhance the overall aesthetic.

NLTF

A vibrant festive video project for the Philippine Department of Tourism - Region 3 and their event - North Luzon Travel Fair, showcasing the unique highlights and attractions of each city within the region.

Role

Video Editor, Animator

Responsibilities

Video Editing and Animation

For the project with the Department of Tourism Region 3, the initial focus was on animating the logo using After Effects and Premiere Pro. Given the logo's complex elements and the tourism context, the goal was to create a festive animation that maintained a smooth flow without becoming overwhelming.
